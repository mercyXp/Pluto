# Pluto Hackathon Demo Script

## Opening

Crypto wallets still force people to think in wallet addresses, hashes, and confusing interfaces. Pluto makes Solana payments feel like a conversation.

## Step 1

Open Pluto.

Show that it opens directly to the voice/chat screen after onboarding or Demo Mode.

## Step 2

Say or type:

```txt
Send 0.1 SOL to Muape for lunch.
```

## Step 3

Pluto responds:

```txt
I found two Muapes. Do you mean Muape K ending k9f2 or Muape A ending 2p91?
```

## Step 4

Choose Muape K.

## Step 5

Show the confirmation screen.

Say:

```txt
Pluto never sends funds without a clear confirmation.
```

## Step 6

Hold to confirm.

## Step 7

Show the success receipt with transaction hash.

## Step 8

Swipe down to reveal Home.

Show updated activity.

## Step 9

Say or type:

```txt
Request 0.5 SOL from Alex for dinner.
```

## Step 10

Show the request QR/payment link.

## Closing

Pluto combines ElevenLabs voice AI with Solana payments to make crypto feel simple, safe, and human.
